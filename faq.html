How do I start the buying process?

Share your goals (purpose, budget, timing, and preferred regions). We’ll pair you with a GRH advisor who curates listings, arranges viewings, and coordinates negotiations and closing.

Do you work with international buyers?

Yes. GRH specializes in cross-border transactions, guiding clients on residency, ownership structures, and compliance with local laws.

Can I purchase in my local currency?

Transactions usually settle in the seller’s currency. We connect clients with FX partners for conversions and better transfer rates.

Do you assist with financing and mortgages?

We maintain relationships with lenders worldwide to help with mortgages, appraisals, and documentation.

What fees should I expect at closing?

Expect costs such as transfer taxes, legal fees, inspection reports, lender charges, and GRH’s brokerage commission.

What about taxes for non-residents?

Tax obligations vary by country. We can connect you with international tax advisors to ensure compliance.

Do I need a local attorney or notary?

Most jurisdictions require one or both. GRH can recommend verified professionals for contract and title checks.

How does GRH verify property information?

We conduct title checks, seller disclosures, third-party inspections, and market comparisons for accuracy.

Can I tour properties remotely?

Yes — GRH offers live video tours, annotated floor plans, and neighborhood overviews worldwide.

Do you offer rental and property management?

Yes, GRH provides leasing and management through our offices or trusted partners in many regions.

Can GRH help me sell a property?

Absolutely. We handle valuation, staging, global marketing, and tailored transaction strategies.

How do I contact an advisor?

Use our Contact Us page or email advisors@grh.global.
